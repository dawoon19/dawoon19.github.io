Illustrations

The 17th Season (2018)

Featured in the 2017-2018 Carillon Yearbook

This was a collaborative project digitally illustrated in the iOS software ibisPaintX for a spread in the 2017-2018 yearbook for Bellarmine College Preparatory High School which had the theme of "Seasons". The accompanying poem (not pictured here) was written by the photo editor at the time, who used each season as a symbol for the cycles of joy, challenges, and triumphs we will encounter in our lives. The drawing represented the message visually in a spread acting as a sendoff to seniors.
